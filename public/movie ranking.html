The Best Movies According to Deepanshu

My Top 3 Movies of all time

3 Idiots

This comedy-drama revolves around the friendship of three engineering students at an Indian engineering college and explores themes of pursuing passion over societal pressure and the flaws in the Indian education system.

Dangal

This biographical sports drama follows the journey of wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat, who trains his daughters Geeta and Babita to become world-class wrestlers, challenging societal norms along the way.

Lagaan: Once Upon a Time in India

This epic sports drama is set in colonial India and tells the story of a group of villagers who challenge British rulers to a game of cricket (Lagaan) to escape oppressive taxes, blending sports, drama, and patriotism.
